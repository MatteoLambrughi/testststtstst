CHORUS

Through a game to unwind social pressure

Going to Karaoke bar is an important social activity for young people in Taiwan. However, singing in front of the crowd could be stressful. “CHORUS” is an add-on service in Karaoke bar which aims to relieve participants’ pressure and engage them by playing a game.

Year
2017
Category
Service design
Game design
Type
Personal Project

Design Process

this is an excellent company! I personally enjoyed the energy andthe professional support the whole team gaveto us into creating website.

Design Challenge

How can we improve the user involvement in the Karaoke bar to create a more engaging experience?

Empathize

Collect users‘ opinions about ”singing in KTV with a crowd” and find their needs.

Define

Use ”Empathy Map” to understand user’s perspectives, identify the main problem and how it could be changed to 3 aspects.

Main Problem : People go to KTV for entertaining, but some of them feel embarrassed, alienated and unwilling to sing. However, they still want to fit in a group.

Support

HMW ”singing along”, instead of singing alone?

Standard

HMW change the only standard ”singing beautifully” to other ways?

Purpose

HMW create another purpose to let you sing “naturally” rather than sing ”actively” ?

Ideate

Brainstorm different types of elements in games to fulfill user’s needs.

Brainstorm

Brainstorm different games and rules such as, guessing the name, take turns to finish the song, talent show and song challenging.

User Feedback

Conduct users online session around topics, for example: “Is it easy to play?” “Does it sounds interesting ?” ”Is it easy to understand?”

Idea Selection

Base on the interviewer ’s opinions to select 4 elements to develop the game ”cooperation”, ”singing”, ”competition”, and “simple”

Prototype & Test

Develop a paper prototype and conduct the user testing in its context
